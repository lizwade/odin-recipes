Tuna Lasagne

Description

This easy tuna lasagne with courgette, pepper and ricotta will become your budget family dinner of the season.

The ricotta saves time making a white sauce and makes it extra delicious.

Ingredients (for 6 people)

  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 courgettes, coarsely chopped
  • 1 red pepper, deseeded, coarsely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed or chopped
  • 400g can diced tomatoes
  • 250ml (1 cup) passata
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 425g canned tuna (3 tins) drained, flaked into large pieces
  • 500g smooth ricotta
  • 1 lemon, rind finely grated
  • 1 egg, lightly whisked
  • 200g grated three cheese blend (ideally cheddar, mozzarella and parmesan)
  • 5-6 lasagne sheets
  • Handful of fresh parsley

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C/170°C fan. Lightly grease a 19 x 25cm (base measurement) baking dish. Heat the oil in a large, deep frying pan over medium heat. Add the onion, courgette and pepper. Cook, stirring often, for 5 minutes or until soft. Add the garlic and cook, stirring, for 1 minute or until aromatic.
  2. Stir in the tomato, passata, and oregano. Season. Add the tuna and gently fold to combine.
  3. Combine the ricotta, lemon rind and egg in a bowl. Season with salt and stir until smooth. Add half the grated cheese.
  4. Cover the base of the prepared dish with a layer of lasagne sheets, trimming to fit. Spread one-third of the tuna mixture over, then top with another layer of lasagne sheets. Repeat with the remaining tuna mixture and lasagne sheets, finishing with a layer of lasagne sheets.
  5. Spread the ricotta mixture over the top of the lasagne. Sprinkle with parsley leaves and the remaining grated cheese. Bake for 40-45 minutes or until golden. Set aside for 10 minutes to cool slightly before serving.
